the subject

Sometimes chance intervenes before shooting the camera. We stir water and oil to form bubbles or throw piles of shavings on the floor. Sometimes we get good results.

moving subject

By combining long exposure with movement we can paint with light. The trajectories of the objects are difficult to control so we have to take many shots to get the one we are looking for.

moving camera

When objects don't move you can move the camera, of course! Movement is relative. So we can draw strokes and even join things that were not together in the same photo.
