random paths

It can be drawn by walking randomly. You can also ask the computer to draw the random path made by hundreds of colored dots. The computer will generate random numbers to draw (as if it were throwing dice, but thousands of times).

decision making

Will any drawings be formed? You can decide how many points you will use. You can also decide how many groups of points there will be when the computer starts drawing. You can also choose between different algorithms.
