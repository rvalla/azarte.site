strange attractors

There are systems of equations that behave in unpredictable ways. We can use them to draw. We draw thousands of points that sometimes end up all lumped together in one place, and sometimes they draw what we call a strange attractor.

equations

In a strange attractor the equations define the location of each point starting from the previous point, recursively. It is impossible to predict in advance how it will look. Very small changes in the values of the coefficients of the equations can produce completely different drawings.
