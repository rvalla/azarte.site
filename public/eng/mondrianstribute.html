Piet Mondrian

Piet Mondrian was born in 1872 in Amersfoort. He is considered one of the most important artists of last cetury. Perhaps his most famous paintings are those full of colored rectangles (which have even been transformed into dresses).

collaborative pieces

I invite you to participate in the creation of a collaborative work in his homage. To participate you will have to play a very simple game. The game has 4 levels and when you finish each one you will get a Mondrian's strip. I'm going to mix a lot of them to make some art works.

You have to send me your strips (by mail o through telegram). You will receive a digital copy of the work in which you have participated.

the game

The game consists of chasing four tunes. After clicking to start the program you will be able to start playing the melodies. The only thing you have to do is click every time you hear a sound. The images are downloaded automatically. Remember to send them to me! And share the game with your friends so we can pay a good tribute to Mondrian.
